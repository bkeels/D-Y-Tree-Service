WHO WE ARE

Rooted in Excellence

Founded in 2023, D&Y Tree Service brings together decades of combined arboricultural expertise with a fresh, customer-focused approach. We're not just another tree service - we're your neighbors, committed to enhancing the beauty and safety of Southeast Michigan's urban forest.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide tree care services that combine technical excellence with old-fashioned customer service. Today, that vision has grown into a thriving business serving hundreds of satisfied clients across the region.

"We measure our success not just by the trees we care for, but by the relationships we build with each client."

— David & Yvonne, Founders
